A few things about me

I am a software developer born in '87 and living in Braunschweig, Germany.

In 2010 i started working at GESIS Gesellschaft für Informationssysteme mbH, the IT subsidiary of german steel concern Salzgitter AG.

My background is in server-side applications with PHP and MySQL using LAMP stacks, although i am currently focusing on more "hip" web techniques such as AngularJS and Node. As for native app development i am looking into Swift on both the iOS and the OS X platform. My first project was an unofficial Runtastic scrapper and an OS X app for the RocketBeans.TV Live Channel on Twitch, both written in Swift.
